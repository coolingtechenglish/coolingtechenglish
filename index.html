Facebook Helps Creators Report Fake Accounts
Facebook 幫助創作者檢舉冒充帳號
Facebook wants to help its users more. Many creators see fake accounts of themselves. Now, it is easier to report these bad people. The company is also making new rules. These rules stop people from stealing other people's posts. It makes the site safer for everyone.
Facebook 為了保護用戶，簡化了檢舉冒充帳號的流程。同時，平台也制定了新規則，禁止未經授權的轉發內容，確保創作者的權益。
📰 TechCrunch Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Creator A2 創作者
A person who makes or builds something new.
“The content creator posted a new video today.”
Report A2 檢舉
To tell an authority about a problem or bad behavior.
“Please report the fake account to the website.”
Rule A1 規則
An instruction that tells you what you can or cannot do.
“There is a new rule for all users.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Make it easier 使其更容易
“The new button will make it easier to share photos.”
Fake account 假帳號
“Do not trust a fake account that asks for money.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is Facebook making easier for creators?
Reporting impersonators.
Q2: Are the rules about content becoming stricter?
Yes, they are tightening the rules.
Entertainment
A2
Spotify Lets Users Change Their Music Taste
Spotify 讓用戶調整音樂喜好設定
Spotify has a new feature for its users. People in New Zealand can test it now. Users can see their own Taste Profile. They can change the music settings if they want. This helps the algorithm pick better songs for them.
Spotify 正在紐西蘭測試一項新功能，允許用戶直接查看並修改自己的「音樂喜好設定檔」，讓演算法推薦更準確的歌曲。
📰 The Verge Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Feature A2 功能
A special part of a product or service.
“This app has a great new feature for offline listening.”
Change A1 改變
To make something different.
“You can change your password in the settings.”
Pick A2 挑選
To choose something from a group.
“I will pick a new song for the playlist.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Taste profile 喜好設定檔
“My taste profile shows that I like jazz music.”
Ease up 放輕鬆、減輕
“Please tell the algorithm to ease up on the pop music.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Where can users test this new feature first?
In New Zealand.
Q2: What can users do to their Taste Profile?
They can view and make changes to it.

Microsoft Brings AI Assistant to Xbox Consoles
微軟將 AI 助理引入 Xbox 主機
Microsoft is adding its Copilot AI assistant to current Xbox consoles this year. An official from the company shared this news at a conference. The AI is designed to help players while they are gaming. It will also be available on other services soon. This is a big step for gaming technology.
微軟宣布將在今年把 Copilot AI 助理帶到現有的 Xbox 主機上，這項功能旨在為玩家提供遊戲協助，未來也將擴展至更多服務。
📰 The Verge Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Assistant B1 助理
A person or program that helps someone with their work.
“The AI assistant can answer many questions.”
Console B1 遊戲主機
A special computer designed for playing video games.
“I bought a new game console for my home.”
Conference B1 研討會
A large meeting where people discuss a specific topic.
“Many experts attended the technology conference.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Current-generation 當代、最新一代
“The game works best on current-generation hardware.”
Gaming AI 遊戲人工智慧
“Gaming AI is changing how we play video games.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the name of the AI assistant coming to Xbox?
Copilot.
Q2: Where did the Xbox product manager announce this news?
At a panel at the Game Developers Conference (GDC).

A Look at the Apple Studio Display XDR
Apple Studio Display XDR 螢幕評測
The Apple Studio Display XDR is a very high-quality monitor. It is great for people who need perfect colors for work. However, the screen is quite expensive for most users. Many other monitors now have similar features for less money. It is an excellent product, but it might be too much for some people.
Apple Studio Display XDR 是一款高品質螢幕，色彩精準且適合專業工作，但其昂貴的價格與市場上其他競爭產品相比，可能並不適合所有使用者。
📰 Wired Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Monitor B1 螢幕
A screen that shows computer information or video.
“I need a bigger monitor for my design work.”
Expensive A1 昂貴的
Costing a lot of money.
“This camera is too expensive for me.”
Quality B1 品質
How good or bad something is.
“The picture quality of this screen is amazing.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Hefty cost 高昂的費用
“The new laptop comes with a hefty cost.”
Not enough 不足夠
“The battery life is not enough for a full day.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Who is the Studio Display XDR good for?
People who need perfect colors for work.
Q2: What is one reason some people might not buy it?
It is very expensive.

Nvidia’s Strategic Pivot Toward CPUs in AI Development
Nvidia 在 AI 開發中向 CPU 轉型的戰略佈局
Nvidia is preparing for a significant shift at its upcoming GTC conference, emphasizing the growing importance of CPUs alongside its traditional GPU dominance. As the demand for agentic AI rises, CEO Jensen Huang is expected to introduce specialized processors optimized for complex AI tasks. This strategic move highlights a broader trend in the tech industry where hardware must evolve to support autonomous agents. By focusing on CPUs, Nvidia aims to stay ahead in the competitive race against rivals like AMD. This pivot marks a critical investment of $20 billion in future-facing AI technology.
Nvidia 即將在 GTC 大會上展示其策略轉變，除了傳統的 GPU 外，將更強調 CPU 在代理型 AI 中的核心作用，以應對市場對更複雜運算處理器的需求。
📰 CNBC Tech Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Strategic B2 策略性的
Relating to the identification of long-term goals.
“The company made a strategic decision to enter the new market.”
Dominance B2 優勢、支配地位
Power and influence over others.
“Their market dominance has lasted for over a decade.”
Autonomous B2 自主的
Able to function without outside control.
“The lab is working on autonomous robots for mining.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Take center stage 成為焦點
“Sustainability will take center stage at the conference.”
Poised to 準備好、即將
“The startup is poised to change the industry.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is Nvidia emphasizing alongside GPUs at the GTC conference?
CPUs.
Q2: What kind of AI is driving the demand for these new processors?
Agentic AI.
