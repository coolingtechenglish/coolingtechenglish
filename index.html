OpenAI Buys a Finance Startup
OpenAI 收購了一家金融新創公司
OpenAI recently bought a company called Hiro. Hiro is a small startup that works with personal finance. Now, OpenAI owns this company to use its technology. They want to add new tools to ChatGPT soon. These tools will help people plan their money and budgets. People use ChatGPT to write text or code every day. Soon, it will also give advice on how to save money. This is a big step for the AI company. They want to be useful for many different parts of your life. More people will use the app for banking and finance now. The teams at Hiro will join OpenAI to build these new features. It is an exciting time for everyone who uses AI tools. We will see these changes in ChatGPT in the near future.
人工智慧巨頭 OpenAI 近期收購了個人理財新創公司 Hiro。此舉顯示 OpenAI 希望將金融規劃功能整合進 ChatGPT。未來用戶將能透過 AI 獲得更專業的財務建議。這項合作將提升 ChatGPT 的實用性與服務範圍。Hiro 團隊將加入 OpenAI 以開發這些新功能。
📰 TechCrunch Apr 14, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Acquisition B1 收購
The act of buying a company or property.
“The acquisition of the startup cost a lot of money.”
Startup A2 新創公司
A new company that is just beginning to operate.
“He works at a small tech startup in the city.”
Finance A2 金融
The management of large amounts of money.
“She studies finance to understand how banks work.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Bring into 引入
“OpenAI will bring new finance tools into the app.”
Part of 一部分
“Financial planning is now part of their service.”

Q2: What will happen to the Hiro team?

Microsoft Stops the Outlook Lite App
微軟將終止 Outlook Lite 應用程式
Microsoft has some news about its software today. They will stop the Outlook Lite app next month. This app started back in the year 2022. It was made for phones that have very little storage space. It also worked well in places with slow internet speeds. Now, the company thinks it is time to end this project. They want to focus on their main Outlook app instead. Users should prepare to move their data soon. Most people will switch to the regular version of the app. This change is part of Microsoft's plan to clean up their services. They want to make sure all users have a good experience. If you still use Lite, you should update to the new app. The company says this is the right time to say goodbye.
微軟宣布將在下個月正式終止 Outlook Lite 應用程式。該軟體自 2022 年推出，旨在協助硬體規格較低或網路環境較差的地區用戶。然而，微軟決定將資源集中在主要應用程式上。目前已建議用戶盡早轉換使用一般版本。此舉是為了優化並整合該公司的軟體服務。
📰 TechCrunch Apr 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Retirement B1 退休/退役
The end of the use of a product or service.
“The software is scheduled for retirement next month.”
Storage A2 儲存空間
The amount of space on a device for files.
“My phone does not have enough storage for photos.”
A2 版本
A form of something that is slightly different from other forms.
“Please download the newest version of the app.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Designed for 為...而設計
“The app was designed for slower internet connections.”
Focus on 專注於
“They want to focus on the regular Outlook app.”

Q2: When will the app stop working?

You Can Buy a Humanoid Robot
你現在可以購買人形機器人
A company called Unitree is selling a robot. You can find this robot on AliExpress right now. It is a humanoid robot, which means it looks like a person. The price for this robot is about 4,370 dollars. It can move in many different ways like a person. It has some skills, like doing small tricks or exercises. The company is selling it to people all over the world. Many people wonder what a person will do with this robot at home. Right now, it is an entry-level machine for people who like technology. It is a very interesting piece of hardware for the home. We will see if more people buy these robots in the future. The technology is moving very fast today. Would you like to have a robot in your own living room?
Unitree 公司宣布在 AliExpress 上架販售人形機器人 R1。這款機器人售價約為 4,370 美元，具備靈活的活動能力。目前消費者可以輕鬆在國際平台上購買這項產品。然而，對於該機器人的居家實用性，業界仍有許多討論。這是科技業中又一項令人矚目的家用硬體發展。
📰 Wired Apr 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Humanoid B1 人形的
Having an appearance or character resembling a human.
“The humanoid robot can walk like a man.”
Market A2 市場
A place or area where goods are bought and sold.
“They are bringing the robot to the global market.”
Entry-level B1 入門級的
Suitable for someone who is just starting to use something.
“This is an entry-level camera for new students.”
💬 Useful Phrases
All over 遍及各處
“They are selling the robots to people all over the world.”
Move fast 進步快速
“Technology is moving fast in the modern world.”

Microsoft Raises Prices for Surface
微軟調漲 Surface 系列價格
Microsoft changed the prices for their Surface computers. The Surface Pro and Surface Laptop now cost more money. This is because there is not enough RAM for all computers. RAM is a part that helps the computer work very fast. Because there is a shortage, parts are more expensive now. The computers cost 500 dollars more than before. The starting price moved from 999 to 1,499 dollars. Many people were surprised by this big price increase. Microsoft stopped selling the cheaper versions last year. This change affects many new buyers who need a new laptop. You will need to save more money to buy these new machines. The tech industry has many problems with parts lately. Hopefully, the prices will go down again in the future.
由於全球記憶體 (RAM) 短缺，微軟宣布調漲 Surface Pro 與 Surface Laptop 的價格。新款裝置的起價從 999 美元提升至 1,499 美元，漲幅達 500 美元。這項變動反映了硬體供應鏈所面臨的成本壓力。對於近期有換機需求的消費者而言，這是一個不小的負擔。目前科技產業仍持續受到零組件供應不穩的影響。
📰 The Verge Apr 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Shortage B1 短缺
A situation where there is not enough of something.
“There is a global shortage of computer chips.”
Increase A2 增加/調漲
To make something larger in amount or price.
“They decided to increase the price of the computer.”
Global A2 全球的
Relating to the whole world.
“This is a global problem that affects many companies.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Cost more 價格更高
“These new laptops cost more than the old ones.”
Not enough 不足
“There is not enough RAM to build all the computers.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Why did Microsoft increase prices?
Q2: How much did the price go up?

Federal Charges Filed After Attack on OpenAI Headquarters
針對攻擊 OpenAI 總部的聯邦指控
Daniel Moreno-Gama currently faces federal charges following a violent attempt to target OpenAI’s headquarters and the residence of CEO Sam Altman. Prosecutors claim the suspect traveled from Texas to California with the explicit intent to end Altman’s life. The incident culminated on April 10th when Moreno-Gama allegedly threw a Molotov cocktail at Altman’s private home. Shortly thereafter, he attempted to gain unauthorized entry into the OpenAI headquarters in San Francisco. Authorities successfully intercepted the suspect, and a search of his possessions uncovered documents detailing his fears regarding the potential extinction of humanity due to AI development. This alarming event highlights the escalating physical dangers faced by leaders in the rapidly advancing artificial intelligence industry. Law enforcement agencies continue to investigate the extent of his plans as federal prosecutors pursue the case. The tech community remains shocked by the severity of this targeted security breach.
Daniel Moreno-Gama 因企圖攻擊 OpenAI 總部及執行長 Sam Altman 的住宅，目前正面臨聯邦指控。檢方指出該嫌犯從德州前往加州，並攜帶自製燃燒彈進行破壞，意圖對 Altman 不利。警方在逮捕過程中發現嫌犯持有關於「AI 可能導致人類滅絕」的極端文件。此事件震驚了科技界，並突顯出 AI 領域高層面臨的安全威脅。目前聯邦檢察官正持續針對此案件進行調查。
📰 The Verge Apr 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Prosecutor B2 檢察官
A lawyer who conducts the case against a person accused of a crime.
“The prosecutor presented evidence in the federal court.”
Unauthorized B2 未經授權的
Without official permission or approval.
“He tried to gain unauthorized access to the building.”
Extinction B2 滅絕
The state or process of a species or group dying out completely.
“The suspect feared the extinction of humanity due to AI.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Face charges 面臨指控
“The suspect will face federal charges for his crimes.”
Targeted security breach 針對性的安全漏洞/入侵
“The company tightened its security after the targeted breach.”

Q2: What actions did the suspect take on April 10th?
