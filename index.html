Microsoft changes the Windows taskbar
微軟更改 Windows 工作列
Microsoft is changing the Windows 11 taskbar. Users can now move it to the top or sides. This was not possible for five years. Many people asked for this change. It makes the computer easier to use.
微軟終於允許 Windows 11 用戶將工作列移動到螢幕的頂部或側邊。這項功能在五年後重新回到作業系統中。
📰 The Verge Mar 20, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Move A1 移動
To change the position of something.
“Please move the box to the desk.”
Possible A2 可能的
Able to be done or achieved.
“It is now possible to finish the work today.”
Change A1 改變
To make something different.
“We need to change the settings.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Bring back 帶回 / 恢復
“The company decided to bring back the old design.”
In response to 回應 / 針對
“They made the change in response to user requests.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Can users move the Windows taskbar now?
Yes, they can.
Q2: How long did it take for this change to happen?
It took almost five years.
Hardware
A2
Nintendo Switch 2 will have a new battery
任天堂 Switch 2 將配備新電池
Nintendo will change the Switch 2 for the European Union. The new battery will be easy to replace. The old battery was stuck inside the device. This follows new rules that start in 2027. This helps make the device last longer.
為了符合歐盟的新規定，任天堂計畫推出可更換電池版本的 Switch 2。這項改變將讓電池變得更容易替換。
📰 The Verge Mar 20, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Replace A2 更換
To take the place of something old.
“I need to replace my broken phone.”
Current A2 目前的
Happening or existing now.
“The current version is very popular.”
Follow A2 遵循
To obey or go after a rule.
“We must follow the school rules.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Stuck inside 卡在裡面 / 固定在裡面
“The battery was stuck inside the case.”
Go into effect 生效
“The new law will go into effect next year.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Why is Nintendo changing the battery design?
To comply with new EU rules.
Q2: When do the new EU rules start?
They start in February 2027.
Software
B1
Microsoft reduces AI features on Windows
微軟減少 Windows 上的 AI 功能
Microsoft is removing some Copilot AI features from Windows apps. Previously, the AI was integrated into tools like Photos and Notepad. The company wants to reduce the amount of extra software. This decision aims to make the system run better for users. It shows Microsoft is listening to feedback about software bloat.
微軟正在減少 Windows 應用程式中的 Copilot AI 功能。此舉是為了改善系統效能並回應使用者對於軟體過於臃腫的批評。
📰 TechCrunch Mar 20, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Remove B1 移除
To take something away.
“They decided to remove the extra buttons.”
Integrated B1 整合的
Combined into a whole.
“The software is integrated into the system.”
Feedback B1 回饋意見
Opinions about a product or service.
“We appreciate your feedback on the new app.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Roll back 撤回 / 回退
“The team had to roll back the latest update.”
Aims to 旨在 / 目標是
“The project aims to help more students learn coding.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is Microsoft doing with its AI features?
They are reducing them in some Windows apps.
Q2: Why is Microsoft making these changes?
To improve performance and reduce software bloat.
Cybersecurity
B1
Tumblr users worried by sudden bans
Tumblr 用戶因突如其來的封鎖感到擔憂
Many Tumblr users were surprised when their accounts were banned on Wednesday. An automated system caused these sudden account restrictions. Many of the affected users identify as transgender women. People are now asking questions about how Tumblr handles moderation. The company is currently under pressure to explain these actions.
Tumblr 的自動化系統近日錯誤地封鎖了許多帳號，其中包含許多跨性別女性用戶，引發了對於平台審核機制的擔憂與批評。
📰 The Verge Mar 20, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Automated B1 自動化的
Operated by machines without people.
“The factory uses automated systems.”
Affect B1 影響
To cause a change in something.
“The new rules will affect many people.”
Explain A2 解釋
To make something clear or easy to understand.
“Please explain why the system failed.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Under pressure 承受壓力
“The manager is under pressure to finish the task.”
Handle moderation 處理審核
“The company needs a better way to handle moderation.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What caused the Tumblr account bans?
An automated system caused the bans.
Q2: Who seemed to be hit hard by these bans?
Users who identify as trans women.
AI
B2
Nvidia's AI gaming tech faces criticism
Nvidia 的 AI 遊戲技術面臨批評
Nvidia’s latest AI upscaling technology, DLSS 5, is encountering significant backlash from the gaming community. Many players describe the visual output as uncanny and aesthetically unappealing, despite Nvidia's ambitious claims about performance. While developers express skepticism regarding the necessity of this technology, industry analysts suggest it might eventually become the industry standard. This situation highlights the ongoing tension between raw AI processing power and genuine user experience. As the technology matures, both players and studios remain wary of its long-term impact on gaming fidelity.
Nvidia 的最新 AI 遊戲技術 DLSS 5 因視覺效果不佳而遭到玩家強烈抵制，儘管 Nvidia 對其寄予厚望，但開發者與玩家對於這種依賴 AI 處理圖像的趨勢仍持保留態度。
📰 Wired Mar 20, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Backlash B2 強烈反對
A strong negative reaction by a large group of people.
“The company faced a backlash after the price increase.”
Aesthetic B2 美學的
Related to the appearance or beauty of something.
“The game has a unique aesthetic style.”
Skepticism B2 懷疑態度
A doubt about the truth or value of something.
“The public showed skepticism toward the new plan.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Industry standard 產業標準
“This technology is quickly becoming the industry standard.”
Remain wary 保持警惕
“Consumers remain wary of data privacy issues.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: How do gamers feel about DLSS 5?
They find it uncanny and off-putting.
Q2: What is the long-term outlook for this technology according to some analysts?
It may eventually become the industry standard.
