E-commerce
A2
Amazon charges sellers more for shipping
亞馬遜向賣家收取更多運輸費
Amazon now charges sellers a fuel fee. This is because of the war in Iran. Energy prices are very high now. Amazon calls this fee temporary. They do not know when it will end. Many sellers are not happy about this cost.
由於伊朗戰爭導致能源價格飆升，亞馬遜開始向賣家收取額外的燃油附加費。該公司表示這是一項臨時政策，但目前尚未確定具體的結束時間。
📰 TechCrunch Apr 02, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Surcharge B1 附加費
An extra amount of money that you must pay.
“The hotel added a small surcharge for breakfast.”
Temporary A2 暫時的
Not lasting for a long time.
“This is only a temporary job for me.”
Global A2 全球的
Relating to the whole world.
“Global warming is a big problem.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Call for 要求
“The situation calls for immediate action.”
Drive up 推高（價格）
“Higher demand will drive up the price.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Why is Amazon charging sellers more money?
Because energy prices are high due to the war in Iran.
Q2: Is the new fee permanent?
No, Amazon calls it a temporary fee.
Privacy
A2
Check your privacy on the Granola app
檢查你的 Granola 應用程式隱私設定
Granola is an app for taking notes. It uses AI to help you work. You should check your settings now. Anyone with a link can see your notes. Also, the app uses your notes to train AI. You can choose to stop this in the settings.
Granola 是一款 AI 筆記應用程式，但預設隱私設定可能會讓有連結的人看到你的筆記。該應用程式也會利用你的內容進行 AI 訓練，使用者應檢查設定並選擇關閉。
📰 The Verge Apr 02, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Privacy A2 隱私
The state of being alone or keeping things secret.
“You should protect your online privacy.”
Default B1 預設
What happens if you do not change anything.
“The default setting is to share notes.”
Internal B1 內部的
Inside a company or organization.
“They keep their notes for internal use.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Double-check 再次確認
“Please double-check your work before sending it.”
Opt out 選擇退出
“You can opt out of the email list.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: Who can see your notes if you do not change the settings?
Anyone who has the link.
Q2: Does Granola use your notes for AI training?
Yes, unless you choose to opt out.

Microsoft touts AI progress despite market pressure
微軟儘管面臨市場壓力仍宣傳 AI 進展
Microsoft executives are aggressively promoting the sales traction of their Copilot AI tool, aiming to soothe investor anxiety regarding the product's performance. While analysts observe that the adoption of the 30-dollar-per-month service remains in its early stages, the company is doubling down on its AI strategy. This push comes as the company deals with significant skepticism from the market about long-term profitability. By highlighting growth, leadership hopes to stabilize the stock price after a period of intense analyst pressure. The success of this initiative is crucial for Microsoft's competitive standing in the rapidly evolving AI software industry.
微軟高層積極宣傳 Copilot 的銷售表現以緩解投資者焦慮。儘管分析師認為此服務仍處於發展初期，但微軟持續加強其 AI 策略，以應對市場對其長期獲利能力的質疑。
