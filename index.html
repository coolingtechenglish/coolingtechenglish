Facebook Helps Creators Report Fake Accounts
Facebook 協助創作者檢舉冒充者
Facebook is changing how it works for creators. Many people pretend to be famous creators online. This is called impersonation. Now, Facebook makes it easier to report these fake accounts. The company also has new rules for copied content. These changes help creators protect their brand.
Facebook 正在改變創作者的使用方式，以解決冒充他人帳號的問題。現在創作者可以更容易地檢舉這些假帳號，同時平台也加強了對抄襲內容的規範，保護創作者的權益。
📰 TechCrunch Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Creator A2 創作者
A person who makes content for the internet.
“The video creator has many fans on Facebook.”
Fake A1 假的
Not real or true.
“Do not trust that fake account.”
Protect A2 保護
To keep something safe from harm.
“We must protect our personal data online.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Make it easier 讓...更容易
“This app makes it easier to talk to friends.”
Report someone 檢舉某人
“You should report the user if they are mean.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the problem for creators on Facebook?
People are pretending to be them.
Q2: What is Facebook doing?
It is making it easier to report fake accounts.

Microsoft Brings AI to Xbox Consoles
微軟將人工智慧帶入 Xbox 主機
Microsoft plans to add a new AI assistant to its Xbox consoles this year. This assistant is called Gaming Copilot. It helps players while they are playing their favorite games. The company shared this news at a big developer conference. It will soon be available on more services as well.
微軟計畫今年將「遊戲副駕駛」AI 助手導入 Xbox 主機，協助玩家在遊玩時獲得支援。此消息在遊戲開發者大會上公布，未來也將擴展至更多服務。
📰 The Verge Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Assistant B1 助理
Someone or something that helps with tasks.
“The AI assistant answers my questions quickly.”
Console B1 遊戲機
An electronic device used to play video games.
“I want to buy a new game console.”
Available A2 可取得的
Ready for use or purchase.
“The new update is available for everyone today.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Get ready to 準備好去...
“We are getting ready to start the meeting.”
Bring to 帶到...
“They want to bring new technology to the market.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the name of the new AI assistant?
It is called Gaming Copilot.
Q2: Where did Microsoft announce this news?
They announced it at the Game Developers Conference.

Amazon Increases Price for Prime Video
亞馬遜調漲 Prime Video 價格
Amazon is changing the price for its video service. People who do not want to see ads must pay more. The price will go up by two dollars every month. Now, users in the US will pay four dollars and ninety-nine cents. Many companies are increasing prices for streaming services lately.
亞馬遜調整了 Prime Video 的訂閱價格，不想看廣告的用戶每月需多付兩美元。美國用戶的新價格為每月 4.99 美元，這反映了近期串流服務漲價的趨勢。
📰 CNBC Tech Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Increase A2 增加/調漲
To make something bigger or higher.
“The store will increase the price of milk.”
Service A2 服務
Help or work provided by a company.
“This internet service is very fast.”
Monthly B1 每月的
Happening or paid once every month.
“I pay my monthly rent on the first day.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Avoid ads 避開廣告
“Many users pay to avoid ads.”
Go up 上漲
“Gas prices continue to go up.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: How much will the price increase?
It will increase by two dollars.
Q2: Who has to pay the higher price?
Users who want to avoid ads.

A2
New Tech Gives AI Human Context
新技術賦予 AI 人類背景知識
A new company called Nyne has a good idea. AI agents often miss important human details. The father and son founders want to fix this. They raised over five million dollars to grow. Their technology helps AI understand people better.
名為 Nyne 的新創公司旨在解決 AI 代理缺乏人類背景知識的問題。由一對父子創辦，該公司已募得超過 500 萬美元，致力於讓 AI 更理解人類需求。
📰 TechCrunch Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Founder B1 創辦人
A person who starts a new company.
“The founder worked hard to build the business.”
Context B1 背景/情境
The situation or information around an event.
“I need more context to understand the story.”
Raise A2 募資/募集
To collect money for a specific purpose.
“The company wants to raise money for new projects.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Father-son duo 父子檔
“The father-son duo runs a small cafe.”
Missing out 錯過
“We are missing out on a great chance.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What problem is Nyne trying to solve?
AI agents are missing important human context.
Q2: Who started the company?
A father and son.
Hardware
B2
Nvidia’s Strategic Pivot at GTC Conference
輝達在 GTC 大會的策略轉向
Nvidia is preparing for a major shift during its upcoming GTC conference. While the company has long dominated the GPU market, there is an increasing emphasis on CPUs for agentic AI tasks. Jensen Huang is expected to reveal specialized processors that bridge the gap between processing power and smart decision-making. This twenty-billion-dollar initiative signals a move to integrate technology from startups like Groq into their broader ecosystem. Analysts believe this pivot will redefine the efficiency of next-generation AI agents.
輝達在 GTC 大會上展現重大策略轉向，除了 GPU 外，更強調用於代理式 AI 的 CPU 技術。黃仁勳預計發表專用處理器，並整合如 Groq 等新創技術，這項 200 億美元的計畫旨在重新定義 AI 的運算效率。
📰 CNBC Tech Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Dominate B2 主導
To be the most important or powerful element.
“Nvidia continues to dominate the AI chip market.”
Initiative B2 倡議/計畫
A new plan or process to achieve a goal.
“This green energy initiative helps the environment.”
Integrate B2 整合
To combine things to form a whole.
“They will integrate the new software into the system.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Take center stage 成為焦點
“Sustainability will take center stage at the meeting.”
Bridge the gap 縮短差距
“New training programs help bridge the gap for workers.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the focus of Nvidia's shift at GTC?
A focus on CPUs for agentic AI tasks.
Q2: What does the 20 billion dollar bet involve?
Incorporating technology from AI startup Groq.
