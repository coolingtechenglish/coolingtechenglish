Facebook Helps Creators Report Fake Accounts
Facebook 幫助創作者檢舉冒充帳號
Facebook wants to keep creators safe. Now, it is easier to report fake accounts. These fake accounts try to copy real people. Facebook also has new rules for copied content. These changes protect people's hard work online.
Facebook 致力於保障創作者的安全。現在，創作者可以更輕鬆地檢舉冒充帳號，保護自己的內容免受他人盜用。
📰 TechCrunch Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Creator A2 創作者
A person who makes new things.
“She is a popular video creator on social media.”
Report A1 檢舉
To tell someone about a bad action.
“You should report the bad user to the app.”
Fake A2 假的
Not real or not true.
“Do not follow that fake account.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Keep safe 保持安全
“We use strong passwords to keep our accounts safe.”
Make it easier 讓...更簡單
“The new update will make it easier to send photos.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is Facebook making easier for creators?
Reporting fake accounts.
Q2: Does Facebook have new rules for content?
Yes, they have new rules for unoriginal content.

Xbox Will Get a New AI Assistant
Xbox 將推出新的 AI 助手
Microsoft is bringing a new tool to Xbox. It is called Gaming Copilot. It is an AI assistant for players. It will arrive on consoles later this year. The company wants to help players enjoy their games more.
微軟即將在 Xbox 上推出名為「遊戲 Copilot」的 AI 助手。這項服務將於今年稍後登陸主機，旨在協助玩家獲得更好的遊戲體驗。
📰 The Verge Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Tool A1 工具
Something used to do a job.
“This AI tool helps me finish my work fast.”
Assistant A2 助理
A person or thing that helps.
“My phone has a digital assistant to answer questions.”
Console B1 遊戲主機
A machine for playing video games.
“I want to buy a new game console this year.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Later this year 今年稍晚
“We will travel to Japan later this year.”
Bring to 帶入/引入
“They will bring new features to the app.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the name of the new AI tool?
Gaming Copilot.
Q2: When will it be available on Xbox?
Later this year.

A2
Spotify Lets Users Change Their Music Taste
Spotify 讓用戶調整音樂偏好
Spotify has a new feature for users. Now, people can change their Taste Profile. Sometimes the app picks music you do not like. This tool lets you tell the app to stop. Beta testers in New Zealand will try it first.
Spotify 推出了一項新功能，讓用戶可以自行調整「品味檔案」。如果推薦的音樂不符合喜好，用戶可以透過此功能進行修正，目前先由紐西蘭的測試者體驗。
📰 The Verge Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Feature A2 功能
A part of a service or product.
“The new camera feature is very useful.”
Pick A1 挑選
To choose something.
“Can you pick the best song for the party?”
Profile A2 個人檔案
Information about a person's interests.
“Update your profile with your favorite music.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Try out 嘗試
“We should try out this new restaurant today.”
Ease up 放鬆/減少
“You should ease up on the salt when you cook.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What can Spotify users change now?
Their Taste Profile.
Q2: Who will test this feature first?
Beta testers in New Zealand.
Retail
B1
Amazon Increases Price for Ad-free Video
亞馬遜調漲無廣告影音服務價格
Amazon is changing its prices for Prime Video. Users who want to watch shows without ads must pay more. The price will go up by two dollars every month. This brings the total to four dollars and ninety-nine cents. Many people use this service for movies and TV.
亞馬遜宣布調整 Prime Video 的無廣告訂閱費用。使用者每月需多支付兩美元，調整後的月費為 4.99 美元，這項服務目前廣泛用於觀看影視內容。
📰 CNBC Tech Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Increase B1 增加
To make something larger or higher.
“The store will increase the price of milk.”
Monthly B1 每月的
Happening or paid once a month.
“I pay a monthly fee for my gym membership.”
Total B1 總數
The final amount after everything is added.
“The total cost of the trip is five hundred dollars.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Go up 上升
“Prices for gas continue to go up this year.”
Ad-free 無廣告的
“I prefer watching videos on an ad-free platform.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: How much will the monthly price increase for Amazon?
Two dollars.
Q2: Is the price increase for users who watch ads?
No, it is for users who want to watch without ads.
AI
B2
Nvidia Focuses on New AI Chip Technology
Nvidia 聚焦新型 AI 晶片技術
Nvidia is preparing to make a significant shift in its product strategy at the upcoming GTC conference. CEO Jensen Huang is expected to detail new processors specifically designed for agentic AI. This move highlights the growing importance of CPUs alongside GPUs in the artificial intelligence market. The company has invested twenty billion dollars to advance this technology. This initiative aims to address the massive demand for specialized AI hardware.
Nvidia 預計在即將到來的 GTC 大會上調整產品策略，執行長黃仁勳將展示專為「代理式 AI」設計的新型處理器，凸顯了 CPU 在 AI 市場中日益重要的地位，並展現該公司投入兩百億美元以滿足市場對 AI 硬體的龐大需求。
📰 CNBC Tech Mar 13, 2026
📖 Key Vocabulary
Strategy B2 策略
A detailed plan for achieving success.
“The company developed a new marketing strategy.”
Significant B2 重大的
Important and large enough to be noticed.
“There has been a significant change in the market.”
Demand B2 需求
The desire of customers for a product.
“The demand for fast internet is increasing rapidly.”
💬 Useful Phrases
Take center stage 成為焦點
“AI technology will take center stage at the conference.”
Poised to 準備好/蓄勢待發
“The company is poised to launch a new product.”
❓ Comprehension Check
Q1: What is the focus of the new processors Nvidia is developing?
Agentic AI.
Q2: Why is this shift considered significant?
It highlights the importance of CPUs alongside GPUs in the AI market.
